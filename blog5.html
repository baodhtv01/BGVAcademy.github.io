4 ways to help parents discover potential in children easily

Tuesday, August 13th, 2019

It is not difficult for parents to discover their child's potential with the following 4 simple ways!

Let your baby master his wisdom

Even though it is just a child, consider the baby as an independent entity, with its own personality and unlimited natural potential. You should not have thoughts about what others do, you should do the same in educating your children. Because each child has his or her own strengths and weaknesses, it is necessary to give them a free space to develop and express their own strengths, and at the same time discover their limitations through their flaws, from there they will. learn from experience to self-correct.

Encourage strong self-confidence that is "rooted" in psychology

Encouraging your child to say or think, "I can do this" or always have the thought and say to your child, "I can do this" is the right way to lead and nurture. confidence from the baby's heart. Please patiently observe the baby in all activities to discover the potential of the baby. Since then, there is encouragement and encouragement for the baby in specific cases.
Scientists said that when encouraged and praised, the human brain will secrete dopamine, which has a positive effect on the nerve center, causing people to think: "Next time I will do it. be better". For children, this means that they will strive and make more efforts to make their potentials more and more manifest and promote.

Instead of scolding, give positive reviews

The same thing but there can be many different ways, so in some cases your way of doing it is not necessarily correct. Understand this, you should not rush to criticize or scold when your baby has not done or done anything.
According to psychologists, the best way is to firstly analyze and show your baby the advantages of the baby when doing that job, point out the good and bad points (emphasize: "not good", no (meaning bad) in what he just did, eventually indicating that if he does it again he will surely do it and do it better. This approach is similar to the "sandwich" method of Japanese parenting style: to criticize children according to the structure of "praise - criticism - praise / encouragement" (we often follow the structure of "criticism - damn - damn ", a little more progress is" criticism - praise - criticism).

Discover and nurture the potential of children with age

You need to learn about the development process of young children in order to "set" goals for each stage. From 0 - 3 years old is the period when children explore the surrounding world, learn to recognize objects, control limbs ...
Preschoolers are familiar with the environment of listening, reading and writing at home, in which it is necessary to be exposed to many shapes, textures and colors of the same object. From 7 - 11 years old, the brain area managing the baby's motor skills thrives, many children at this age begin to be interested in and passionate about sports. In addition, from the age of 9 onwards logical thinking develops and gradually expands.

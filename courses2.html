Math Logic Classes




Introduce

Thinking math will stimulate both hemispheres of the brain to work together, increasing the range of brain activity so that children can use the full power of the "central processing unit". The BGV gifted training school will bring students a comprehensive development of the brain. At the same time, we will ensure that the children have the best development environment.

Duration of the course


Basic class: 3 months (5 times/week)
Advanced class: 4 months (5 times/week)

The content of the course :

    Basic Class :
  • Measur: Helps children grasp visual knowledge about quantitative values
  • Build the ability to orient and imagine the space for children.
    Advanced Class :
  • Science: Help your child master the foundations of 4 arithmetic operations.
  • Geometry: Raising the child's sense of geometry through learning content.
  • Language: Nurture Listening - Speaking for children. "Listening - Speaking" is an important prerequisite for developing "Reading - Writing" capacity.

The tuition fees of the courses are as follows :

  • For the Basic Class: The course lasts 3 months with tuition of 1000$ and we are committed to the best teaching quality for the children.
  • For the Advanced Class: The course lasts 4-month course with a cost of 2000$, we are committed to the best teaching quality for our children.
